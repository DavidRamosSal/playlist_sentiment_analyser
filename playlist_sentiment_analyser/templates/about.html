About the analyser

This analyser gets track information from Spotify using the Spotipy library and then gets the lyrics from Genius.com using the lyricsgenius library. It detects the language of the lyrics using the Fasttext library and subsequently performs sentiment analysis on the lyrics that are in english leveraging the Natural Language Processing library Spacy.

Natural Language Processing

Features calculated applying NLP to track's lyrics.

Sentiment score

Compound sentiment of a track's lyrics and ranges from -1 (most negative) to 1 (most positive). A score close to 0 suggests a more neutral sentiment. The sentiment score takes into account the intensity and polarity of individual words to provide a comprehensive sentiment analysis result.

Lexical diversity

A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 describing how many unique words there are in a track's lyrics. It is calculated by dividing the number of unique lemmas in a text by the total number of lemmas.

Audio features

Information about a track's audio provided by the Spotify API.

Musical valence

A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 describing the musical positiveness conveyed by a track. Tracks with high valence sound more positive (e.g. happy, cheerful, euphoric), while tracks with low valence sound more negative (e.g. sad, depressed, angry).

Energy

Energy is a measure from 0.0 to 1.0 and represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity. Typically, energetic tracks feel fast, loud, and noisy. For example, death metal has high energy, while a Bach prelude scores low on the scale. Perceptual features contributing to this attribute include dynamic range, perceived loudness, timbre, onset rate, and general entropy.

Danceability

Danceability describes how suitable a track is for dancing based on a combination of musical elements including tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity. A value of 0.0 is least danceable and 1.0 is most danceable.

Acousticness

A confidence measure from 0.0 to 1.0 of whether the track is acoustic. 1.0 represents high confidence the track is acoustic.
